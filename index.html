Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Kerex Nigeria Limited is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with Nigerian Data Protection Regulations.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number)
  • Employment history and professional qualifications
  • Educational background and certifications
  • Resume/CV and cover letters
  • Background check information (with consent)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Recruitment and talent acquisition for oil & gas sector clients
  • Service delivery and workforce management
  • Compliance with DPR, NIPEX, and regulatory requirements
  • Training and professional development programs
  • Payroll, pension, and welfare administration

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Secure storage systems with access controls
  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and compliance checks
  • Staff training on data protection best practices

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at privacy@kerex.ng for any privacy-related inquiries.

Whistleblowing Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Kerex Nigeria Limited is committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity, transparency, and ethical conduct. This Whistleblowing Policy provides a secure channel for reporting concerns without fear of retaliation.

What Can Be Reported

Employees, contractors, and stakeholders are encouraged to report concerns about:

  • Unethical behavior or misconduct
  • Fraud, theft, or financial irregularities
  • Safety violations or HSE breaches
  • Regulatory non-compliance (DPR, NIPEX, etc.)
  • Corruption, bribery, or kickbacks
  • Discrimination or harassment
  • Conflict of interest
  • Any other illegal or unethical activities

How to Report

Reports can be made confidentially through:

  • Email: whistleblower@kerex.ng
  • Phone: Dedicated hotline (contact HR for number)
  • In Person: Schedule confidential meeting with HR Director
  • Anonymous: Reports can be submitted without revealing identity

Protection & Confidentiality

Kerex Nigeria Limited guarantees:

  • Protection against retaliation for all whistleblowers reporting in good faith
  • Confidential handling of all reports
  • Independent investigation of all credible reports
  • Regular updates on investigation progress (where appropriate)
  • No disciplinary action for good faith reporting

Investigation Process

All reports are:

  • Documented and assigned to an independent investigator
  • Investigated promptly and thoroughly
  • Reviewed by senior management and legal counsel
  • Addressed with appropriate corrective actions
